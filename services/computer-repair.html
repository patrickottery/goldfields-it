Computer Repair & Diagnostics

Something's not right with your computer. Before assuming the worst — or replacing it — let me take a look. Most issues are fixable.

Common problems I fix

  • Computer running slow — cleaning up software, optimising Windows or upgrading storage
  • Blue screens, crashes and freezing
  • Virus and malware removal
  • Windows won't start or keeps restarting
  • Overheating — cleaning dust and replacing thermal paste
  • Broken charging ports, screens and keyboards on laptops
  • Failed or failing hard drives
  • Software errors and update failures

How it works

  • I'll diagnose the problem first and tell you what it'll take to fix it
  • You decide whether it's worth repairing or replacing — no pressure either way
  • If it's hardware, I'll source parts at a fair price
  • If it's not worth fixing, I'll tell you honestly
I won't charge you for an hour of "diagnostics" and tell you to replace it. If I can't fix it, you'll know quickly and for minimal cost.
