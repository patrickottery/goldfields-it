Antivirus & Security

Small businesses are a common target for malware, ransomware and phishing attacks. The right protection doesn't have to be complicated or expensive.

What's included

  • Antivirus and endpoint protection across all business devices
  • Removal of existing malware, adware and unwanted software
  • Windows security hardening and update management
  • Firewall review and configuration
  • Multi-factor authentication setup for business accounts
  • Email filtering and spam protection advice
  • Basic staff guidance on spotting phishing and scam attempts

Why it matters

  • Ransomware can encrypt all your business files and demand payment to restore them
  • A compromised email account can expose customer data and damage your reputation
  • Most attacks target businesses that haven't done the basics
You don't need enterprise-level security — you need the right basics done properly. That's what I focus on for small business clients.
