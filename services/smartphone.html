Smartphone Setup

Got a new phone and not sure where to start? I'll transfer everything across from your old one and get it set up exactly how you want it.

What's included

  • Data transfer — contacts, photos, messages and apps from your old phone
  • Account setup — Google, Apple ID, email and iCloud
  • App installation and login for the apps you use
  • WiFi, Bluetooth and home network connection
  • Privacy and security settings — screen lock, Find My Device, backups
  • Connecting to your computer for photos and syncing
  • Patient walkthrough of anything you want to understand

Who this is for

  • Anyone who's just bought a new phone and is overwhelmed by the setup
  • People switching from Android to iPhone (or vice versa)
  • Those who've lost their old phone and need accounts recovered
There's no rush. I'll take the time to make sure you're comfortable with your phone before I leave.
