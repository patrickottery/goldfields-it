Data Recovery

Accidentally deleted files, a failed hard drive or a computer that won't start — don't panic. There's a good chance your data is still recoverable.

What I can help with

  • Accidentally deleted photos, documents and files
  • Files lost after a Windows crash or failed update
  • External hard drives that are no longer recognised
  • USB drives and SD cards that won't open
  • Formatted drives that still hold data
  • Computers that won't boot — recovering files before reinstalling Windows

How it works

  • I'll assess what's recoverable — if nothing can be saved, there's no charge
  • Recovery is attempted using professional software tools
  • Recovered files are returned on a drive or copied back to your computer
Stop using the drive as soon as possible. Every new write to a drive can overwrite the data you're trying to recover. Get in touch quickly.

Lost something important?

Get in touch as soon as you can — acting quickly gives the best chance of recovery.
