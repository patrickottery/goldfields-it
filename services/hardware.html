Hardware Procurement & Deployment

Buying the wrong equipment is an expensive mistake. I'll recommend the right gear for your budget and use case, source it, and have everything configured and ready to work.

What's included

  • Needs assessment — what you actually need vs. what the spec sheets say
  • Hardware recommendations across desktops, laptops, servers and peripherals
  • Sourcing at competitive prices through trusted suppliers
  • Full setup including Windows configuration, accounts and software
  • Data migration from old machines where required
  • Connecting to your network, printers and shared resources
  • Staff handover so everyone knows how to use the new gear

Who this is for

  • Businesses replacing ageing computers or growing their team
  • New businesses setting up from scratch
  • Anyone who wants advice before making a hardware purchase
I don't have brand loyalties or sales targets. I'll tell you what's actually worth buying for your situation — and what's not.
