Data Recovery

Lost or inaccessible data can stop a business in its tracks. I'll assess what can be recovered and get your critical files back as quickly as possible.

What I can help with

  • Failed or failing hard drives and SSDs
  • Accidentally deleted files and folders
  • Corrupted drives or file systems
  • Formatted drives that still hold recoverable data
  • Files lost after a system crash or failed Windows update
  • Recovery from USB drives, SD cards and external storage

How it works

  • Initial assessment to determine what's recoverable — no charge if nothing can be saved
  • Recovery attempted using professional software tools
  • Recovered files delivered on a new drive or transferred back to your system
  • Backup solution recommended to prevent it happening again
The sooner you act, the better. If a drive has failed, stop using it immediately and get in touch — continued use reduces the chance of a successful recovery.
