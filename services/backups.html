Backups

Most businesses don't think about backups until they need them. By then it's too late. A proper backup system is cheap insurance against hardware failure, ransomware or human error.

What's included

  • Assessment of what data needs to be backed up and how often
  • Local backup setup to an external drive or NAS
  • Cloud backup configuration (Backblaze, OneDrive, or similar)
  • Automated scheduling so backups happen without thinking about it
  • Backup verification — making sure restores actually work
  • Offsite backup options for disaster recovery

Why local AND cloud?

  • Local backups are fast to restore from and don't need internet access
  • Cloud backups protect against fire, theft or hardware failure on-site
  • Using both gives you the best protection with minimal cost
A backup that hasn't been tested isn't a backup — it's a hope. I'll make sure your backup actually restores before you ever need it.
