Network Setup & Management

A reliable network is the backbone of any business. Whether you're starting fresh or dealing with drop-outs and dead zones, I'll design and install a setup that actually works.

What's included

  • Site assessment — understanding your layout, number of users and devices
  • Router and switch selection, configuration and installation
  • WiFi design including access point placement for full coverage
  • Separate networks for staff, guests and IoT devices
  • Firewall configuration and basic network security
  • Cabling advice and installation where needed
  • Ongoing monitoring and support as required

Who this is for

  • Small businesses setting up a new office or premises
  • Existing businesses with unreliable WiFi or slow speeds
  • Businesses with multiple staff needing reliable shared access
  • Rural or larger sites needing extended coverage
I work with business-grade equipment where it makes sense — not the cheapest gear from the shelf, but nothing over-engineered either. You get a network that's reliable, secure and easy to manage.
