Smart Home & IoT Setup

Smart home devices are great in theory — until you can't get them to connect, talk to each other or stay online. I'll get everything set up and actually working.

What I can set up

  • Smart TVs — streaming apps, accounts and remote control setup
  • Streaming devices — Apple TV, Google Chromecast, Roku, Fire Stick
  • Smart speakers — Google Home, Amazon Echo, Apple HomePod
  • Smart lighting, plugs and thermostats
  • Security cameras and video doorbells
  • Network-based media servers (Plex, NAS)
  • Keeping IoT devices on a separate network to protect your other devices

Common issues I fix

  • Smart devices that won't stay connected to WiFi
  • Devices that won't talk to each other or to voice assistants
  • Smart TVs that are slow, crash or have app problems
  • Security cameras that don't record reliably
Smart home tech is also a personal hobby — I use it myself and genuinely enjoy setting it up. You're in good hands.
